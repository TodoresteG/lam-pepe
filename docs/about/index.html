Our History

Lam Pepe was founded in 2012 as a children attraction company. 3 years later the landscape has changed dramatically - our services evolved and we established a fast food restaurant as well. Our clients needs have become more complex, consumer behaviour has changed - and we have changed. That's why after 2 more years we opened a laundarette with the special feature - carpet cleaning. Yet 8 years ago Lam Pepe was started with the belief that our services should make people feel peacefull and relaxed - and we still believe that today.
